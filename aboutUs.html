About Us

Fit Store - Founded by Tarick Joseph in 2022 out of the love of sneaker culture and art. We started out as a small botique in Cape Town, and then ventured out to Durban and then Johannesburg. With the rise in sneaker stores around the world, we then decided to step into the online market in 2015. Today we stock nothing but the latest in premium sneakers and products for sneaker heads around the world. We are sneaker culture.
